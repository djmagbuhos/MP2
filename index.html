About Us

PlayStation World, the trusted online retailer of Playstation consoles and playstation gadgets and accessories in the Metro Manila. Our mission is to provide our customers with an unparalleled selection of Playstation products and outstanding service nationwide. To fulfill this commitment, we continuously innovate and improve our user-friendly website and soon to have our own mobile app. We offer multiple convenient payment methods, brand-certified warranties, efficient fulfillment branches, and a comprehensive shopping platform, ensuring a seamless and satisfying shopping experience for our valued customers.

We firmly believe in keeping up with our innovative approach to deliver a seamless online shopping experience while ensuring top-notch quality products and services to our customers.
